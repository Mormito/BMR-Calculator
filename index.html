Mifflin-St Jeor Equation

Male → BMR = 10W + 6.25H - 5A + 5

Female → BMR = 10W + 6.25H - 5A - 161


Revised Harris-Benedict Equation

Male → BMR = 13.397W + 4.799H - 5.677A + 88.362

Female → BMR = 9.247W + 3.098H - 4.330A + 447.593


W is body weight in kg | H is body height in cm | A is age.

BMR - This is the amount of energy (calories) your body needs to maintain basic life-sustaining functions while at rest, such as breathing, circulation, and cell production.


TDEE - Total Daily Energy Expenditure. This is the total number of calories your body burns in a day, including physical activity. It represents the caloric intake needed to maintain your current weight.


Cutting - A diet model to lose weight, reducing body fat and keeping muscle mass.


Bulking - A diet model to gain weight and build muscle mass.


Light and Aggressive - Represents how much intense is the diet.


Disclaimer: Aggressive cutting results in massive weight loss, but it's really important to be aware of side effects, you will lose body fat AND muscle mass, because your body is literally trying to survive. An aggressive bulking (popular as "dirty bulk") results in massive weight gain, you will build muscles, but the biggest side effect is a big increase in your body fat.
